About Creative Scrapyard

“Creative Scrapyard is an e-commerce website that aims to be a platform where people can sell their old or scrap items. From our website, an artist can buy those scrap items and customize that scrap items to make useful and decorative items. These creative items can be sold on the website itself and other people can buy these items. It help people to earn by selling their scrap, it help the artists to buy stuffs on cheap rate and earn money by showing their creativity on it and it will also promote the initiative of #VocalForLocal and make the world “a better place” by recycling old items.

How It Works?

Platform For An Independent Artist

An artist can add their arts or products made by them on the platform for selling it. It can be anything; a repaired and decorted mug, a customized soap, sketches or anything that they have touched creatively.

Platform For Users To Earn Through Scrap

Any User can add their scrap or old items on the platform for other users to buy them. An artist can add their arts or products made by them on the platform for selling it. It can be anything; a repaired and decorted mug, a customized soap, sketches or anything that they have touched creatively.

Platform For Turning Best From The Waste

The scrap that is added by a User on Scrapyard can be bought by anyone, maybe another user, maybe a artist, maybe a designer. This scrap will be customized and recycled and turned into something useful. Some best from the waste artist may buy this scrap and turn it into a creative item and sell it on the Creative Store and earn money by selling it.
